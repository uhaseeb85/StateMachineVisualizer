Flow Diagram Builder

📊

Visualize and understand complex process flows

Log Analyzer

📜

Process and analyze large log files with unprecedented speed

Business Value Reduces analysis time from hours to minutes, enables faster incident resolution, and improves system reliability

AI Log Analyzer

🤖

Leverage AI to extract meaningful insights automatically

  • Automatic pattern recognition identifies recurring issues

  • Natural language summaries of complex log data
Business Value Proactively identifies lot of potential issues before they impact users
